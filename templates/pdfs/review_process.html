Review process


1. Disclose other experimental conditions (if there were any).

2. Disclose deception (if there was any).

3. Correct any misconception that the participant might have if you are aware of any.

4. List your hypotheses (if applicable).

5. Remind about the right to retract data now (if applicable, payment still is due).

6. Provide a website with the description of the research project and other relevant resources (if applicable).

7. Provide your business card / contact information.

8. DO NOT talk of some patterns of the studied behavior being more plausible (or expected) than others.
